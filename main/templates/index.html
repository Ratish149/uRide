Currently, We only accept Khalti Payment

What kind of Car Rental do I need?

To choose the right car rental, consider the number of passengers, luggage, terrain, and your budget. For family trips, opt for an SUV or minivan. For city driving, a compact car works well. Luxury cars suit special occasions, while economy cars are budget-friendly. Also, factor in fuel efficiency and any specific needs like all-wheel drive.
